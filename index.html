  • Fresh, seasonal dishes with locally sourced ingredients.

Why Us

Why Choose Our Restaurant

01

Authentic Nepali Flavors

Durbar Thakali Grill offers an authentic taste of Nepal with its Thakali cuisine, inspired by the native tribes of Tukuche. You can savor the genuine and ever-changing flavors of Nepal in every dish.

02

Seasonal Freshness

We take pride in using fresh, locally sourced ingredients, ensuring that our dishes are not only delicious but also reflect the current season. This commitment to freshness guarantees a unique and delightful dining experience.

03

Warm and Intimate Atmosphere

At Durbar Thakali Grill, we believe in the power of connection and meaningful conversations. Our restaurant provides an intimate and relaxed atmosphere where you can enjoy memorable evenings filled with great food.
